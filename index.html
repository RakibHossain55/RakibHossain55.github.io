Welcome

I am Md Rakib Hossain, a PhD candidate in the Department of Materials Science and Engineering at the University of Florida and an Assistant Professor of Physics at Gopalganj Science and Technology University, Bangladesh. My academic background is in physics, and my current work focuses on computational materials science, machine-learning interatomic potentials, phonon-dislocation interactions, and thermal transport.

Current Research Focus

  • Machine-learning interatomic potentials for PbSe, PbTe, and PbSe/PbTe heterostructures
  • Phonon-dislocation interactions and their effects on thermal transport
  • First-principles and atomistic modeling of materials behavior
