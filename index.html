Research Highlights

A modern snapshot of current and past work, from nanomaterials and adsorption studies to interatomic potentials and phonon-driven transport.

Current

Machine-learning interatomic potentials

Developing and evaluating potentials for PbSe, PbTe, and related heterostructures to study lattice dynamics and thermal transport with atomistic efficiency.

Current

Phonon-dislocation interactions

Investigating how structural defects scatter phonons and influence conductivity, with a focus on physically grounded simulation workflows.

Previous

Low-dimensional nanomaterials

First-principles studies of sensing, adsorption, and delivery behavior in boron-based and other nanoscale materials systems.

Welcome
