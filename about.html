Academic background, teaching experience, and the path from physics to computational materials science.

Biography

Md Rakib Hossain is a PhD candidate in the Department of Materials Science and Engineering at the University of Florida. He is also an Assistant Professor of Physics at Gopalganj Science and Technology University, Bangladesh, currently on education leave while pursuing doctoral research.

His research interests include computational condensed matter physics, density functional theory, atomistic simulation, thermal transport, and machine-learning interatomic potentials.
