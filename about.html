About

Md Rakib Hossain is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Physics at Gopalganj Science and Technology University, Bangladesh. He is currently on education leave and pursuing his PhD in the Department of Materials Science and Engineering at the University of Florida under the supervision of Dr. Simon Phillpot.

He completed his Bachelor of Science and Master of Science in Physics at Jahangirnagar University, Bangladesh. His earlier research centered on first-principles studies of low-dimensional nanomaterials, including their interactions with drug molecules and toxic gases. His doctoral work now focuses on computational materials science, especially machine-learning interatomic potentials and phonon thermal transport in semiconductor systems.
